Enrollment Process

How to Enroll Your Child at Moses Academy

We welcome new students throughout the year. Follow these simple steps to begin your child's educational journey with us.

1

Visit the School

Schedule a campus tour, meet with admissions staff, view facilities and classrooms, and ask questions about our programs.

2

Obtain Application Form

Get the form at our school office, download from website, or request via email or WhatsApp.

3

Submit Documents

Complete application form, birth certificate, passport photos, previous report cards, and medical records.

4

Entrance Assessment

Age-appropriate assessment in English and Mathematics, plus interview for older students.

5

Receive Admission Offer

Acceptance letter issued with fee structure, terms and conditions, and enrollment deadline.

6

Complete Enrollment

Pay fees, purchase uniform, receive student ID card, and attend orientation program.
