Welcome Message

Welcome to Moses Academy School

At MOSES ACADEMY, we inspire, build, and develop civilized and highly motivated future leaders for a better world. We are delighted to welcome you to our educational family and trust that you will find this comprehensive resource helpful in understanding our institution.

We are a small, friendly, and successful Private School, happily situated within the grounds of Deep Eye Water Community, Western Area Rural District, Sierra Leone.

Our Mission

We strive towards building the physical, cognitive, social, and emotional intelligence and intellect of children.
