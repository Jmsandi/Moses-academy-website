Scholarship Programs

Moses Academy is committed to providing educational opportunities for all deserving students, regardless of their financial background. Our scholarship programs support both students and teachers.

Scholarship for Less Privileged Kids and Orphans

We believe every child deserves access to quality education. Our scholarship program provides full and partial financial support to academically talented but economically disadvantaged students, including orphans and vulnerable children.

Program Benefits:
  • Full or partial tuition coverage
  • Free textbooks and learning materials
  • School uniforms and supplies
  • Meal support programs
  • Academic mentoring and counseling
  • Extra-curricular activity support
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Good academic performance or potential
  • Strong character and discipline
  • Commitment to education
  • Priority given to orphans and vulnerable children
How to Apply:

Interested students or guardians should:

  1. Complete the scholarship application form
  2. Submit academic records and recommendation letters
  3. Provide proof of financial need
  4. Attend an interview (if shortlisted)

Professional Development for Teachers through Scholarship

Investing in our teachers means investing in our students' future. Our teacher professional development scholarship program supports educators in advancing their skills, knowledge, and qualifications.

Program Benefits:
  • Funding for advanced degrees and certifications
  • Professional training workshops and seminars
  • Educational conferences and symposiums
  • Subject-specific skill development courses
  • ICT and modern teaching methodology training
  • Research and innovation support
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Currently employed at Moses Academy
  • Minimum 2 years of teaching experience
  • Demonstrated commitment to professional growth
  • Strong teaching performance record
  • Willingness to share knowledge with colleagues
Impact on Education Quality:

Teachers who participate in our professional development program:

  • Bring innovative teaching methods to classrooms
  • Improve student engagement and learning outcomes
  • Mentor and train other teachers
  • Contribute to curriculum development
  • Enhance the overall quality of education
