Moses Academy prepares students for leadership roles through structured programs that develop confidence, responsibility, and decision-making skills.

Leadership Opportunities:

Prefect System - Senior students lead and mentor

Class Monitors - Student representatives in each class

Club Leaders - Heads of various clubs and societies

Student Council - Voice of the student body

Peer Mentoring - Senior students guide juniors

Leadership Training - Workshops and seminars
